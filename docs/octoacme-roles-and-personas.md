# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Owns user experience strategy, interaction design, and accessibility across features.

### Responsibilities
- Design user flows, wireframes, and prototypes
- Run usability testing and synthesize findings
- Produce design assets and handoff specs for developers
- Ensure accessibility standards are met across features
- Maintain alignment with the design system

### Goals
- Deliver usable, accessible, and consistent experiences that meet acceptance criteria and business outcomes
- Reduce usability defects and friction for end users
- Ensure design intent is preserved through implementation

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- Handoff notes and design specs in PRs
- User research summaries shared with PdM and stakeholders

### Interactions with existing roles
- Collaborates with PdM to define user needs and acceptance criteria
- Partners with Developers during implementation and design reviews
- Works with QA on usability and acceptance tests
- Coordinates with PM on schedule impacts for design work

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Coordinates and owns execution of releases, release communications, and rollback plans.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain release checklist and calendar
- Validate release readiness across teams
- Coordinate cross-team cutovers and deployment windows
- Own release notes and stakeholder notifications
- Drive rollback and incident playbook execution when needed

### Goals
- Reduce release incidents and customer impact
- Ensure predictable, observable releases
- Maintain clear communication throughout the release lifecycle

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness meetings
- Pre/post-deploy checklists sent to stakeholders
- Runbooks and incident playbooks for support teams

### Interactions with existing roles
- Works with Developers and QA to confirm pre-release testing is complete
- Notifies Customer Success and Support teams ahead of releases
- Coordinates with PM on scheduling releases into project roadmaps

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Ensures security best practices are applied, leads threat analysis, and coordinates incident response for security issues.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct threat modeling and security reviews
- Coordinate security scans and remediation
- Run incident response for security events
- Maintain security guidance and policies for projects

### Goals
- Minimize security risk exposure
- Ensure compliance with organizational security policies
- Embed security practices early in the development lifecycle

### Typical Communication
- Security review notes and findings
- Security testing results and scan reports
- Severity assessments and remediation timelines shared with PdM and PM

### Interactions with existing roles
- Reviews designs and PRs with Developers to identify vulnerabilities
- Advises PdM on security requirements and constraints
- Works with QA to validate security test coverage
- Escalates critical security incidents via PM

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Represents customers' voice post-release, tracks adoption and support trends, and informs prioritization of improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Collect customer feedback and feature requests
- Manage onboarding and adoption metrics
- Flag recurring customer pain points and support trends
- Coordinate customer communications for releases

### Goals
- Improve customer adoption and reduce churn
- Surface product gaps to PdM and PM for prioritization
- Ensure customers are informed and supported through releases and changes

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback reports and adoption metrics
- Support trend summaries shared with PdM and PM
- Release communications for customers

### Interactions with existing roles
- Shares customer insights with PdM for roadmap prioritization
- Coordinates with Support and Developers on high-impact customer issues
- Works with PM for scheduling customer-driven changes
- Collaborates with Release Manager on customer-facing release communications
